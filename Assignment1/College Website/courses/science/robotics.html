Robotics Course

Description

Robotics involves the design, construction, and programming of robots to perform tasks autonomously or assist humans in various applications. From industrial automation to healthcare and exploration, students in robotics explore concepts in mechanics, electronics, and artificial intelligence to create innovative robotic systems.
