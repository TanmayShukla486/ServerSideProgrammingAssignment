Civics Course

Description

Civics is the study of the rights and responsibilities of citizens within a society and the functioning of governmental systems at local, national, and global levels. Students in this field explore topics such as democracy, citizenship, political institutions, and public policy, gaining a deeper understanding of the mechanisms that shape civic life and governance structures. Through critical analysis and engagement with contemporary issues, students develop the knowledge and skills necessary to actively participate in civic affairs and contribute to the democratic process.
