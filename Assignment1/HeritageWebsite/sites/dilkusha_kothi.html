Dilkusha Kothi

Description

The best thing about the monuments of Lucknow is their diversity as these are built as per not just one or two, but several styles of architecture from all over the world. While the above monuments reflected Mughal, Hindu, European and other styles, Dilkusha Kothi is here to show you the magnificence of the Baroque style of architecture.

History

Saadat Ali Khan, during his rule in the 19th century, constructed this building, which is a complete replica of Seaton Delavel, an English country house built in 1721. The sprawling greenery surrounding the edifice adds to its charm.
