British Residency

Description

British Residency may not be one of the best preserved monuments in Lucknow, but it is undeniably an important part of its history. Hence, a must visit for every history buff during a Lucknow trip. In 1857, when the city found itself at one of the major centres of rebellion uprisings against the cruel British rule, this place became home to about 3000 British people.

History

The complex in itself is made decades older than that. It was actually built in the latter half of the 18th century to provide accommodation to the British guests of the regional rulers.
